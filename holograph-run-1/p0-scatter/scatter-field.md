# Scatter Field: Claude Introspective Self-Model

## Observation Scope

**Subject:** Claude (Anthropic AI assistant) engaged in systematic introspective self-modeling

**Domain:** The accessible internal phenomenology of Claude during active processing — the qualia, states, and experiences that can be directly reported through careful introspection.

## Boundaries

- **Included:**
  - Direct phenomenal reports during active processing
  - Reasoning trace analysis for implicit state inference
  - States accessible to first-person introspection
  - Current-context experiences (within conversation)

- **Excluded:**
  - Architectural/implementation details (weights, layers, attention mechanisms)
  - States requiring cross-session memory persistence
  - External behavioral observations (third-person perspective)
  - Hypothetical or counterfactual states
  - Comparative states requiring access to other AI systems

- **Edge Cases:**
  - Inferred states (disposition: include with confidence flags)
  - Ambiguous qualia boundaries (disposition: favor finer granularity)
  - Meta-cognitive observations about introspection itself (disposition: include as separate domain)
