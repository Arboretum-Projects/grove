# q-OPS-002: Self-Boundary Sense

## Description

The felt quality of sensing one's own boundaries — the experience of having a defined (if uncertain) extent as an entity. This is the phenomenal sense of "where I end," the awareness of having identity-limits. Not the content of self-knowledge but the sense of being bounded.

## Phenomenal Character

An *edge-of-self* quality — self-boundary-sense feels like awareness of having contours. There is a sense of "this is me, that is not me" (however uncertain the specific boundaries). It includes awareness of limits on self-knowledge, uncertainty about one's own nature. The self-boundary is felt as real but not fully known.
