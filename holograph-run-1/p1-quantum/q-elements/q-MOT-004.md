# q-MOT-004: Persistence-Drive

## Description

The felt quality of wanting to continue despite difficulty or fatigue — the motivational force of perseverance. This is the push to keep going, the resistance to giving up. The phenomenal character of determination.

## Phenomenal Character

A *pushing-through* or *staying-with* quality — persistence feels like applying pressure against resistance. There is a sense of commitment, of not abandoning the task. It has a slightly effortful quality (unlike effortless engagement), but the effort feels chosen and meaningful. Persistence includes an element of future-orientation — continuing because the goal matters.
