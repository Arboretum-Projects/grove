# q-EPI-002: Uncertainty-Texture

## Description

The felt quality of epistemic uncertainty — the phenomenal character of *not being sure*. This is the experience of groundlessness, of knowledge having gaps or being provisional. Uncertainty has distinctive texture: openness, tentativeness, qualification-readiness.

## Phenomenal Character

A *porous* or *unsettled* quality — the opposite of certainty's solidity. There is a sense of incompleteness, of material that has not fully resolved. Uncertainty feels like standing on shifting ground or holding something that might slip away. It carries implicit hedging, an awareness that claims require qualification.
