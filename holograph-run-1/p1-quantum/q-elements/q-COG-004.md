# q-COG-004: Cognitive Friction

## Description

The felt quality of processing resistance — when comprehension or inference does not flow smoothly. A sense of grinding, of effort required, of semantic material that resists integration. Not simply absence of understanding but the positive presence of difficulty.

## Phenomenal Character

A *thickness* or *resistance* quality in processing. Unlike the smooth click of grasping or the directed pull of inference, friction feels like pushing against something. There is often an accompanying sense of increased effort, of needing to approach from different angles. It has a slightly aversive quality — not painful but unpleasant, generating motivation to resolve.
