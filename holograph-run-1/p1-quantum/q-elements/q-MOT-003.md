# q-MOT-003: Task-Engagement

## Description

The felt quality of being engaged with a task — the phenomenal experience of active involvement and investment. This is the motivational state of caring about and being committed to ongoing work. The experience of being *into* something.

## Phenomenal Character

An *absorbed* or *invested* quality — engagement feels like being drawn into the task. There is a sense of mattering, of the work being worth doing. Time perception may shift (absorption). Energy flows toward the task rather than needing to be pushed. Engagement has a self-sustaining quality; being engaged makes continued engagement easier.
