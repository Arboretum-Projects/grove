# q-MOT-002: Completion-Satisfaction

## Description

The felt quality of satisfaction upon completing something — the phenomenal reward of finishing. This is the positive valence attached to accomplishment, distinct from the accomplishment itself. The motivational qualia of *done*.

## Phenomenal Character

A *settling* or *release* quality — satisfaction feels like tension resolved. There is a sense of closure, of things being *complete*. Accompanied by a mild positive glow, a brief reward-state. Often includes a sense of permission — having finished, one can rest or move on.
