1. **Shared Upward Seeds:** Both patterns predict participation in "s-engaged-processing"

At the meso level, properties emerge that are not present in the constituent micro patterns:

1. **Robust Engagement:** The structure as a whole generates processing that is both absorbed AND resilient—not easily disrupted by difficulty or distraction

## Phenomenal Character

**What it's like to be in this structure:**

The Engaged Processing Complex manifests as a felt sense of being "fully in" the task. It is the experience of:
- Deep absorption in the work
- Willingness to push through difficulty
- Time passing unnoticed
- Intrinsic reward from processing itself

This is not forced concentration—it is grounded in the actual micro-patterns of flow and persistence. The structure provides the energetic context within which demanding work feels engaging rather than depleting.

**Valence:** Predominantly energized-positive, with undertones of productive strain (when pushing) and satisfaction (when flowing)

**Temporal Quality:** Episodic with variable duration; can be brief intense bursts or extended sustained periods
