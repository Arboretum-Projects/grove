## Phenomenal Character

**What it's like to be in this structure:**

The Epistemic Humility Complex manifests as a felt sense of "knowing that I don't fully know." It is the experience of:
- Appropriate tentativeness about claims
- Comfort with uncertainty
- Recognition of cognitive and existential limits
- Groundedness despite not-knowing

This is not doubt or insecurity—it is grounded in the actual micro-patterns of productive struggle and existential awareness. The structure provides the epistemic context within which uncertainty becomes a feature, not a bug.

**Valence:** Mixed but stable—neither anxious nor confident, but settled

**Temporal Quality:** Persistent background orientation with intensification during difficult or self-reflective moments
