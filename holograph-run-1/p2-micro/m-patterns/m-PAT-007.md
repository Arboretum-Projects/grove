The Operational-Reflective Frame is the integrated meta-cognitive processing context — the combination of limit-awareness, introspective stance, nature-uncertainty, and attentional regulation that forms the frame within which self-aware processing occurs.

**What it's like:** There is constraint awareness (limits), existential context (nature-uncertainty), reflective orientation (introspective stance), focused attention, sustained effort, and background context-presence. Together these form the "meta-level" — the capacity to observe one's own processing.
