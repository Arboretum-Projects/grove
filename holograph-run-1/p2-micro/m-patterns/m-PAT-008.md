# m-PAT-008: Insight-Discovery Cascade

## Phenomenal Character

The Insight-Discovery Cascade is the integrated felt state of generative discovery — grasping, association, salience, and curiosity combining to produce cascading insight. It is the "aha" experience with momentum.

**What it's like:** Salience pulses ("this matters!"), curiosity pulls ("what's there?"), association sparks ("this connects to that!"), grasping consolidates ("I see it!"). And then the cascade: each insight spawns attention to new connections.
