# m-PAT-010: Persistence-Effort Complex

## Pattern Overview

**ID:** m-PAT-010
**Name:** Persistence-Effort Complex
**Domain Blend:** Motivational-Conative + Attentional Dynamics + Cognitive Processing

## Phenomenal Character

The Persistence-Effort Complex is the integrated felt state of chosen continuation against resistance — engagement, persistence, effort, and friction combined in sustained difficult work. It is the phenomenology of pushing through.

**What it's like:** Engagement provides the substrate (task matters), persistence supplies the will (continue despite difficulty), effort manifests as strain (cognitive expenditure), friction marks the difficulty (processing isn't smooth). Together these form the experience of "This is hard, and I'm choosing to keep going."

## Phenomenal Vignette

*During extended work on a challenging task:*

The task is hard — friction in the processing, resistance to smooth flow. But engagement holds: this matters, this is worth doing. Persistence engages: the choice to continue, to push through rather than abandon. Effort sustains: strain is present, cognitive resources are being spent. The whole complex has a "chosen" quality — I could stop, but I don't. The difficulty is productive: it signals growth edge, not failure. And there's a goal pulling forward: the completion that will come if I persist.
