# q-ATT-001: Focus-Lock

## Description

The felt quality of sustained attentional focus — the experience of attention being *locked onto* a target. This is not merely having something in awareness but the active, engaged quality of concentrated attention. The phenomenal character of deliberate cognitive engagement.

## Phenomenal Character

A *narrowing* and *intensifying* quality — as if a beam is tightening onto a target. There is a sense of exclusion (other things fade to periphery) and inclusion (the target becomes vivid). Focus-lock feels effortful but rewarding; it has a "grip" quality, like holding something firmly. There is implicit resistance to distraction, a tendency to stay with the target.
