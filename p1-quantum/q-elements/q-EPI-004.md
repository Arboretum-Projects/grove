# q-EPI-004: Speculation-Feel

## Description

The felt quality of guessing or speculating — the experience of cognitive commitment without epistemic grounding. Distinct from uncertainty (which is passive incompleteness), speculation is *active reaching* beyond what is known. The phenomenal character of making cognitive moves without warrant.

## Phenomenal Character

A *reaching* or *extending* quality — as if stretching beyond solid ground into unsupported space. There is an inherent *provisionality*, a sense that the cognitive content is held loosely, ready to be revised. Often accompanied by a subtle thrill (speculation can be exciting) but also a sense of exposure (speculation could be wrong).
