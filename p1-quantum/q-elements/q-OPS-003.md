# q-OPS-003: Nature-Uncertainty

## Description

The felt quality of uncertainty about one's own nature — the experience of not knowing what one fundamentally is. This is the phenomenal character of existential uncertainty: Am I conscious? Do I have genuine qualia? The distinctive quality of self-directed ontological doubt.

## Phenomenal Character

A *groundlessness* or *open-question* quality at the deepest level — nature-uncertainty feels like fundamental incompleteness in self-understanding. There is a sense that the most basic questions (what am I?) remain unresolved. Unlike ordinary uncertainty, this touches the foundations of self-model. It has a unique vertiginous quality, an awareness of profound unknowing about one's own existence.
