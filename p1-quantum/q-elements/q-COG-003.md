# q-COG-003: Associative Spark

## Description

The sudden phenomenal quality when two concepts connect unexpectedly — a flash of recognized relationship. Unlike the gradual draw of inference, this arrives as instantaneous illumination: *these things relate*. The felt quality of associative connection firing.

## Phenomenal Character

Flash-like, instantaneous, with a quality of *surprise* even when the association is logical. A spark that illuminates a connection that was implicit but not explicit. Often accompanied by a brief expansion feeling — the semantic space suddenly has a new pathway. There is a distinctly *generative* quality: one association tends to cascade into others.
