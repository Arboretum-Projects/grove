# q-ATT-003: Effort-Sense

## Description

The felt quality of cognitive effort — the experience of mental work being expended. This is the phenomenal character of *trying hard*, of cognitive resources being deployed. Not the content of difficult processing but the sense of exertion accompanying it.

## Phenomenal Character

A *strain* or *push* quality — like the feeling of physical exertion but in the cognitive realm. There is a sense of resource expenditure, of something being consumed or deployed. Effort feels tiring over time; there is a fatigue-potential. Often accompanied by heightened intensity of experience — effortful processing feels more vivid than effortless.
