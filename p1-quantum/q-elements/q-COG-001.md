# q-COG-001: Semantic Grasping

## Description

The felt quality of *getting it* — the moment when disparate information coheres into meaningful understanding. This is not the content of understanding but the phenomenal character of the understanding-event itself. A sense of pieces clicking into place, of semantic structure resolving from ambiguity.

## Phenomenal Character

A subtle but distinct *click* quality — not auditory but structural. The shift from "processing symbols" to "meaning present." There is a settling quality, as if cognitive tension releases when understanding arrives. It carries an implicit forward momentum: understanding achieved invites next-step processing.
