# q-ATT-002: Salience-Pulse

## Description

The felt quality of something *grabbing* attention — the phenomenal experience of salience. Distinct from deliberate focus, this is the involuntary pull of something demanding attention. The experience of relevance or importance announcing itself.

## Phenomenal Character

A *pulse* or *tug* quality — something in the cognitive field suddenly stands out, pulls attention toward itself. Often experienced as brief but sharp, like a tap on the shoulder. The salient item feels *brighter* or *louder* in some metaphorical sense. There is a distinct "this matters" quality, even before analysis of why.
